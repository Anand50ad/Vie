“We can’t help everyone, but everyone can help someone.”

Vie provides you the platform to help others or to get help from others.

We act as a meadiator between the donor and the donee to eliminate the hassle of both the parties to reach out to each other, i.e., we collect from the donors and deliver to the donees. Both parties can access this feature by just writing a email to us in the specified format.
